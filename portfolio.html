Adventure Quest Status: Final CSS Cleanup

Adventure Quest is a two person cooperative async turn based adventure game. The style of play is similar to Dungeons & Dragons where players face turning points in the story, make choices as to how to proceed, and a random dice roll determines which player's choice wins. The winning choice either leads to success or peril for both of the players. Adventure Quest also incorporates live chat between the players, and game rooms so that multiple games can be ongoing simultaneously.


Tech used: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, NodeJS, Express, Handlebars, mySQL with Sequelize ORM, Materialize, Socket.io, other NPM packages (Body-Parser, Cookie-Parser, Morgan).

Oh, The Places We Will Go! Status: Completed

An app to get ideas for things to do around a city, or any specific location. User can “shop” images of POIs around a location, and if something strikes their interest they can then get further information on location, and a weather forecast, to aid in decision whether to visit. Design in a kid friendly style to encourage children to get involved in organizing family plans.


Tech used: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Google Maps JS API, Google Maps Geocoding API, Google Places API, Google Static Maps API, Weather Underground API, Foursquare API.
