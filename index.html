On the path to becoming a full-stack web developer, I have utterly fallen in love with software development. What other profession allows you to imagine a world in your head, and then bring it to life using no more than a small electronic device and the power of your thoughts, then tuck it away into no space at all only to bring it out again the next day to improve on it even more?

I see how technology is changing the world. I was in college during the early days of the internet, when the world wide web was just getting started. I have seen how ubiquitous it has become with everyone reaching for a tablet, smart phone, or computer as part of their daily routine. Despite that, I really think we are still in the early stages of an explosion of web services and mobile applications that will empower people to have better lives in ways that are only just becoming apparent. I absolutely need to be a part of it and add to it.
